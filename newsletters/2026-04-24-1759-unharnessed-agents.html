Arcturus Labs – Unharnessing our Agents

I just published a new post about how we are thinking too narrowly about agents. Even the phrase "agent harness" nudges us toward treating them as coding tools trapped inside IDEs, when they are really becoming the building blocks for much broader AI products.

The big opportunity is to standardize how we build agents and their components, then let them follow us across the software we use: carrying context, tools, memory, skills, and interfaces wherever work happens.
