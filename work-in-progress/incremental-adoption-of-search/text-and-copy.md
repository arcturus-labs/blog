## Description

What if your e-commerce search team was an AI agent that understood natural language the way a human expert would?

In this lightning lesson, John Berryman (Arcturus Labs) walks through the incremental adoption of agentic search — from traditional Elasticsearch to a full conversational search assistant — without throwing away your existing stack or surprising your users.

Key topics:
- Why RAG and agentic search aren't black boxes — they're transparent pipelines
- How LLM agents handle misspellings, synonyms, and structured filtering out of the box
- The inner loop vs. outer loop: multi-query strategies and conversational UX
- Why agentic search is now more accessible than classical ML for small and mid-size teams
- A 3-stage UI/UX roadmap for safely introducing AI search without disrupting users
- Challenges: index awareness, non-determinism, latency, and cost — and how to address them

Includes a live demo using real estate search as an e-commerce proxy.

Co-presented with Doug Turnbull (Turnbull Maven) — https://maven.com/p/d58da6/incrementally-adopting-ai-in-e-commerce-search

More at https://arcturuslabs.com
