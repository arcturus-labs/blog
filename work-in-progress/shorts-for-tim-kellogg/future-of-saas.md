# The Future of SaaS

**Core idea:** SaaS companies are treating AI agents as a new feature to bolt on, but the real disruption is happening underneath them — IT shops are building bespoke internal tools and becoming the primary competitor.

---

## Quote Sequence (~50 sec)

**[00:19:41]**
> "It's scary times for these traditional SaaS companies."

**[00:20:22]**
> "Agents fit really well into that model — it's just a new feature. You swap it on, sell it, new SKU, more revenue rolls in. But it's not actually changing workflows."

**[00:26:30]**
> "If you look at the competition for different SaaS products, you'll see maybe one or two key players still growing their market share. Then the lesser players shrinking dramatically. But then you look at this big new section that's appearing — IT shops building their own software. Over the last six to nine months it's been shifting, and now it's just accelerating."

**[00:27:09]**
> "Internal IT shops are the primary competitor now."

**[00:27:27]**
